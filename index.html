FRESH MEAT IN OPTIMEAL FEED

NEW ZEALAND LAMB

Lamb is considered an unusual meat for the daily diet of animals, therefore feed with its content can be used as a diet to determine the causes of food allergies or protein intolerance of other types of meat.

TROPICAL SHRIMP

Thanks to shrimp, rich in calcium, phosphorus, zinc, potassium, magnesium, B12 and omega-3 fatty acids, the risk of cardiovascular diseases is reduced and bone health is improved. Natural antioxidant "Astaxanthin" protects body cells from damage.

NORWEGIAN SALMON

Salmon meat is dietary and hypoallergenic, it is a source of omega-fatty acids, so the Optimeal diet, created on the basis of salmon, allows you to significantly improve the condition of the skin and coat.

TENDER VEAL

Veal is a very nutritious type of meat in terms of nutritional value, so some cats prefer to eat only veal-based diets, ignoring food containing other types of meat.
